This recipe is straight from Italy! It will take you back to when you were a child eating at your grandma's house.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Lasagna Noodles
  • Meat Suace
  • Ground Beed=f or Italian Sausage
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles).
  3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top.
  4. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
  5. Repeat the same layers two more times.
  6. Top with mozzarella cheese and then cover and bake at 375Â° for 30 minutes.
  7. Remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes. Broil at the end if desired. Let it rest for 15 minutes prior to serving.
  8. Enjoy!
