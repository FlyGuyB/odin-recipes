Fried Rice

Home

The best fried rice you have ever eaten! Tastes like takeout every time.

Ingredients

  • Cooked day old rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables like carrots and peas
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Additional options include oyster sauce, butter, and various proteins like chicken or shrimp.

Steps

  1. Prepare garlic and scallions.
  2. Cook vegetables in vegetable oil.
  3. Add rice and seasonings to the pan.
  4. Scramble eggs separately.
  5. Combine eggs with rice, add sesame oil, and adjust seasoning.
  6. Enjoy!
