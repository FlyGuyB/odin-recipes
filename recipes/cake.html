Chocolate Cake

Home

This Chocalate cake will knock your socks off. Perfectly moist and impossible to resist

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Baking Powder or Baking Soda
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Preheat & Prep: Preheat your oven and prepare your baking pans
  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: Mix the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  3. Add Wet Ingredients: Add the specified wet ingredients (excluding boiling water) to the dry ingredients and beat.
  4. Stir in Boiling Water: Carefully stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin.
  5. Pour & Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pans and bake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool: Let the cakes cool in the pans before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
  7. Frost: Once fully cooled, frost the cake with your favorite chocolate frosting.
  8. Enjoy!
